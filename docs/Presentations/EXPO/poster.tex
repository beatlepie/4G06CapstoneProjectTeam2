    \block{What is CampusConnections?}{CampusConnections is an Android mobile application built using Unity for students or visitors of McMaster University. 
                                       We aim to increase sociability in the campus by giving attendees of McMaster a centralized application to 
                                       discover events and connect with others of aligning interest in the event. We aid in navigation of the campus through 
                                       our map and Artificial Reality (AR) camera, which can display what events or lectures are happening in the area ahead 
                                       or the specific room the camera is pointed at. In addition, searching lectures and events were made easier, allowing 
                                       users to query events by location or from recieving the details from another friend user. 
                                       
                                       This software application will assist its users in discovery of events navigation throughout McMaster to improve their 
                                       experience during their time at campus.}

    \block{Motivation/Problem}{
        \begin{itemize}
